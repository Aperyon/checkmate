Checklists

A checklist is a template. Put together a list of items or tasks that are always required for a particular process.

Runs

Start a new run of your checklist each time, multiple times.

Checklists vs Todo lists

A Todo list is full of tasks that only need to be completed once.


A checklist describes a process that needs to be repeated over and over again.

Who use checklists?

Astronauts

Man never was going to set foot on the Moon without checklists. Each step of a launch of a rocket is carefully crafted.

Doctors

Surgeons must wash their hands before they start an operation. Today this is common sense, but it wasn’t always this way.

Engineers

As magnificent a sky-scraper may look, there is no magic in it. It comes down to the most mundain things, which are put into a process.

...and You, of course

Checklists help you achieve collossial goals. But this doesn’t mean you can’t use it for simple things.

Staff to pack before a trip (ocasional), paying bills (monthly), shopping lists (weekly) or even things you don’t want to forget before leaving your home, like your wallet, keys and phone (daily).
